PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

Welcome to MagdiBakery ("MagdiBakery", "we", "our", or "us").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when using our website.

By creating an account, placing an order, or using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order through MagdiBakery, we may collect:

• First Name and Last Name
• Email Address
• Phone Number
• Shipping Address
• Billing Address (if applicable)
• Account Login Information
• Order and Purchase Information
• Customer Support Communications

We may also automatically collect technical information such as:

• IP Address
• Browser Type
• Device Information
• Date and Time of Website Access

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Create and manage customer accounts
• Process and fulfill orders
• Arrange delivery of products
• Process payments through authorized payment providers
• Send order confirmations and order updates
• Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
• Prevent fraud and unauthorized activity
• Improve the functionality and security of our website
• Comply with legal obligations

3. Payment Information

Payments may be processed through third-party payment providers, including Paymob, banks, card processors, and other authorized payment services.

MagdiBakery does not store complete credit card or debit card information on its own servers. Payment information is handled by the relevant payment provider in accordance with its own privacy and security policies.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell customer personal information.

We may share information with:

• Payment service providers
• Shipping and delivery partners
• Website hosting providers
• Technical service providers assisting in website operation
• Government authorities or regulators when required by law

Information is shared only when reasonably necessary for providing services or complying with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, disclosure, or alteration.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

• Maintain customer accounts
• Fulfill orders and provide services
• Meet legal and accounting requirements
• Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When information is no longer required, it may be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Cookies

MagdiBakery may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality and user experience.

Cookies help us remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, and improve website performance.

Users may disable cookies through browser settings, although certain website features may not function properly.

8. International Orders

MagdiBakery may accept orders from customers located in different countries where permitted by applicable law and operational capabilities.

By using our services, customers understand that information may be processed in jurisdictions different from their own when necessary to complete orders and provide services.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request:

• Access to personal information held by us
• Correction of inaccurate information
• Deletion of personal information where legally permitted
• Restriction of certain processing activities
• Information regarding how their data is used

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

10. Children's Privacy

MagdiBakery services are not intended for children under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time.

Any updates will be posted on this page together with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of the website after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
